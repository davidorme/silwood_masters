Projects at Silwood

Welcome to the project management website for Masters courses at the Silwood Park campus of Imperial College London. This page provides an overview of the courses covered and their project structures. More details of the courses can be found here

The courses are the Master of Science courses in:

  • Ecology, Evolution and Conservation (EEC)
  • Conservation Science (Con Sci),
  • Computational Methods in Ecology and Evolution (CMEE)
  • Ecological Applications (EA)
  • Taxonomy and Biodiversity (NHM MSc)

and the Master of Research courses in:

  • Ecology, Evolution and Conservation (EEC MRes),
  • Computational Methods in Ecology and Evolution (CMEE MRes)
  • Ecosystem and Environmental Change (eeChange MRes)
  • Tropical Forest Ecology MRes
  • Biosystematics (NHM MRes)

Masters students from both Silwood and the NHM will access project proposals on this site. The structure of projects varies between courses, with both project lengths and start times varying. Silwood Masters projects typically last for five months however: the CMEE MRes has a nine month project; the NHM Masters projects last for 3.5 months; and the Tropical Forest Ecology MRes has a 7 month project.
