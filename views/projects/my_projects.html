My Projects

This table shows all of the projects for which you are the lead supervisor. This includes both filled projects - when the student you supervised and the year will be shown - and also open proposals. Students can see all project proposals that were created within 60 days of the start of this academic year. ().

The proposal database should show students the full range of research available through the courses. Please do not use the conceal option to manage your student numbers.

From this page, you have a number of options:

New Proposal
  • You can create a {{=A('completely new proposal ', _href=URL('projects', 'project_details', scheme=True, host=True))}}.
Clone a proposal
  • You can clone any proposal ( ). This will duplicate the proposal and make that copy visible to students. If you clone a filled project, the copy will become an open proposal.
Open Proposals
  • You can edit the details of a proposal ( ). This link is also used to accept a student on to a project.

  • You can toggle the visibility of open proposals to readvertise open proposals from previous years ( ). It is also possible to conceal proposals that become impossible to support ( ), for example due to equipment failure or travel restrictions.

Filled Proposals
  • You can update the title and descriptions of filled projects to reflect changes in the research that a student is following. The student cannot update these details.
