Staff Login

Staff involved in student project supervision and project marking can use this form to log in to see their projects and marking records. We do not use a password based system - when you enter your email, you will be sent an email containing links to your projects and marking. These links are protected by a unique 256 bit token, which need to be used within an hour.

If your email is not recognised, please check the details on {{=A('this page', _href=URL('staff','proposing_projects'))}} on how to get approval to propose and supervise projects.
