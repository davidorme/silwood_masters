Staff Login

Staff involved in student project supervision and project marking can use this form to log in to see their projects and marking records. Enter your email below to be sent a login link. These login links need to be used within an hour.

Note that you need to be both in our staff directory and have been given access to propose and supervise projects before to see these records. Please email us if:

  • your email is not recognised or,
  • you have changed your email address (for example, a move to a new institution), or
  • you have not yet been given access.
