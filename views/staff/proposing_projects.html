Proposing projects

In order to propose and supervise Masters projects, you will need to both in our list of teaching staff and have current approval to supervise projects. We use your email to login in to project proposal and marking pages, so if you are an approved supervisor, simply enter your email {{=A('on this login page', _href=URL('staff','staff_login'))}}.

If you cannot login then:

  1. you may not be on the list of teaching staff,
  2. you are on the list but we have an outdated email address, or
  3. you have not yet been approved as a supervisor.
