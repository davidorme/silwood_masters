Request Staff Access

Staff involved in student project supervision and project marking can use this form to request access to their projects and marking records. Enter your email below and an email will be sent to that address containing a login link. These login links need to be used within an hour.

Note that you need to be both in the system and approved to propose and supervise projects before you will be given access. If your email is not recognised or you have not yet been approved, please email us.
