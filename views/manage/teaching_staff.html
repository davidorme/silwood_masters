Teaching staff

This table shows all of the staff that have ever been associated with Masters teaching, project supervision or marking. You can edit staff members to update email addresses - people often carry on working with us after changing institution - and any other details. There are three flags associated with teaching staff:

  • Can Supervise: Only staff with this flag set are allowed to create, advertise and then supervise research projects.
  • Is Internal: Only staff with this flag set can sole supervise research projects. External supervisors must have an internal supervisor supporting the project.
  • Is Active: You cannot delete members of staff - they will likely be associated with previous teaching and marking in the database - but you can mark them as inactive, which will hide them from view for most users.
